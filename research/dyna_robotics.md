# Dyna Robotics Research

## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  Dyna Robotics develops general-purpose robotic foundation models (e.g., DYNA-1) that enable high-performance robots to perform diverse tasks in commercial environments with 99+% success rates over 24-hour operations. The DYNA-1 model uses a single-weight architecture for embodied AI, supporting applications in precision manufacturing, assembly, folding/garment handling in laundromats, and food service in restaurants. Core model: Develop and deploy AI software for robots, with revenue from commercial contracts; focuses on scaling embodied AI to achieve physical AGI, emphasizing cost-effective hardware (under $50K total ownership) and continuous learning from deployments.

- **Current stage (Series ?, public, revenue if known).**  
  Private, post-Series A. Early commercial stage with DYNA-1 launched in 2025; not public. Revenue not disclosed, but implied growth from seed to Series A (focus on R&D/commercialization).
