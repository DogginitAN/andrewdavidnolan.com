# The Honest Company Research

## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  The Honest Company, Inc. is a digitally-native consumer goods company focused on creating clean, sustainable, effective, and thoughtfully designed personal care products. It manufactures and sells diapers and wipes, skin and personal care, and household and wellness products. The core business model revolves around three segments: Diapers and Wipes (approximately 50% of revenue, $188.45M in 2024), Skin and Personal Care ($79.54M in 2024), and Household and Wellness. Revenue is generated primarily through omnichannel retail sales (87% in 2024 via retailers like Target and Walmart), with a focus on eco-friendly ingredients, transparency, and accessibility to empower healthier living.

- **Current stage (Series ?, public, revenue if known).**  
  Publicly traded on Nasdaq under HNST since its IPO in May 2021. Trailing twelve-month (TTM) revenue as of 2025: $0.38 billion (up 1.26% YoY). Fiscal 2024 revenue: $0.37 billion (up 9.87% from 2023's $0.34 billion). The company is in a transformation phase (Transformation 2.0: Powering Honest Growth), emphasizing profitability and exiting low-margin channels.

- **Recent funding (amount, date, lead investors).**  
  No recent venture funding as a mature public company. The 2021 IPO raised over $100 million. No equity or debt issuances noted in the last 6 months; focus on operational cash flow and restructuring for efficiency.

- **Employee count and growth trajectory.**  
  Approximately 164 full-time employees as of the latest 10-K filing (February 2025 for fiscal 2024). Some sources cite up to 361 including part-time/contract, but full-time figures are stable or slightly declining due to restructuring. Growth trajectory: Revenue growth moderating (FY2025 outlook: -3% to flat overall, but organic +4% to +6%); shifting from expansion to profitability focus, with exits from DTC, apparel, and Canada representing ~22% of Q3 2025 revenue.

- **Headquarters and remote policy.**  
  Headquarters: 12130 Millennium Drive, Suite 500, Los Angeles, CA 90094, USA. Remote policy: Not explicitly detailed, but the culture emphasizes inclusivity, work-life balance, and collaboration; hybrid or flexible arrangements are inferred, with no mandates mentioned. The company supports remote-friendly practices aligned with its values of consciousness and community.

- **Key customers or partners.**  
  Key customers: Major retailers including Target (30% of 2024 retail sales), Walmart (9%), Amazon, and others like Ulta Beauty for beauty expansion. Serves consumers in the US (primary, $378.34M in 2024 revenue), Canada (exiting in 2025), Europe, and China. Partners: Disney and Pixar for licensed kids' personal care collections (e.g., Toy Story bathtime line, September 2025); Lil' Libros for Latin American culture collaboration (August 2024); Baby2Baby for diaper donations.
