# GXO Logistics Research

## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  GXO Logistics, Inc. (GXO) is the world's largest pure-play contract logistics provider, specializing in warehousing, distribution, order fulfillment, e-commerce, reverse logistics, managed transportation, and supply chain optimization. It designs, manages, and optimizes supply chains using advanced technology, automation, robotics, AI, and data analytics to improve efficiency, productivity, sustainability, and visibility. Core model: Outsourced logistics solutions for blue-chip clients, with revenue from multi-year contracts, technology-enabled services, and value-added offerings like automation and reverse logistics; focuses on solving complex challenges for industries such as aerospace, automotive, consumer packaged goods, e-commerce, food/beverage, healthcare, and retail.

- **Current stage (Series ?, public, revenue if known).**  
  Public company (NYSE: GXO, spun off from XPO Logistics in August 2021). Revenue: $11.7B in 2024; Q3 2025 $3.4B (8% YoY growth from $3.2B in Q3 2024).

- **Recent funding (amount, date, lead investors).**  
  No recent private equity funding (public since 2021). Growth through acquisitions (e.g., Wincanton in 2025) and operations. No specific recent funding rounds noted.
