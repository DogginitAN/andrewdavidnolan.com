## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  Fleetio provides cloud-based fleet management software that helps organizations track, analyze, and improve operations for vehicles, equipment, parts, drivers, and more. Key features include maintenance management, inspections, fuel tracking, telematics integrations, and outsourced repair workflows. Core model: SaaS platform with subscription-based revenue (plans like Pro at $5/vehicle/month), focusing on centralizing data for efficiency, compliance, and cost control; recurring from software, plus integrations/partnerships.

- **Current stage (Series ?, public, revenue if known).**  
  Private, post-Series D. Revenue: Estimated $58M annually (2025, based on employee count and average revenue per employee); growing from strong customer adoption (e.g., 45% YoY in 2023).
