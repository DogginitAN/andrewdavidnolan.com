## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  Old Dominion Freight Line, Inc. (ODFL) is a leading less-than-truckload (LTL) motor carrier providing regional, inter-regional, and national LTL shipping services through a single integrated, union-free organization. It consolidates smaller shipments from multiple customers onto one trailer to optimize capacity and costs. Core offerings include expedited transportation, container drayage, truckload brokerage, and supply chain consulting. The business model emphasizes premium service with on-time, damage-free deliveries (over 99% on-time rate, low claims ratio), advanced technology for tracking and efficiency, and a customer-first approach to build long-term relationships, driving recurring revenue from transportation services across manufacturing, retail, and government sectors.
