## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  Super Micro Computer, Inc. (Supermicro, SMCI) is a global leader in high-performance, energy-efficient server and storage solutions, specializing in rack-scale designs optimized for AI, cloud, enterprise IT, HPC, and edge computing. The company provides complete systems (servers, storage, switches, software) with rapid time-to-market innovation, including liquid-cooled AI factories and NVIDIA-integrated GPU platforms (e.g., Blackwell Ultra, Rubin NVL72). Business model: Direct sales of customized, building-block systems to hyperscalers and enterprises, emphasizing vertical integration, green computing, and total cost of ownership savings through energy efficiency.
