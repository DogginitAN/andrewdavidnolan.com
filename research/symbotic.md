## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  Symbotic Inc. (SYM) develops and deploys AI-powered robotic warehouse automation systems, including autonomous mobile robots (SymBots), high-density storage structures, and intelligent software for orchestrating end-to-end supply chain operations. The platform handles pallets, cases, and individual items with 99.9999% accuracy, enabling high-speed navigation (up to 25 mph), improved storage density, and reduced labor costs. Core model: Sell and install hardware systems (e.g., SymBot fleets, modular storage) with recurring revenue from operation services, software maintenance, and support; focuses on transforming warehouses into strategic assets for retailers/wholesalers.

- **Current stage (Series ?, public, revenue if known).**  
  Public (NASDAQ: SYM, listed 2022). Revenue: FY2025 $2.25B; Q1 FY2026 (ended Dec 2025) $630M (29% YoY growth); FY2026 guidance at least $2.6B.
