## Company Profile

- **What does the company do? Core business model.**  
  Crusoe Energy Systems (Crusoe) is a vertically integrated AI infrastructure company that designs, builds, and operates AI factories (data centers) powered by clean, abundant energy sources, including renewables (solar, wind, hydropower, geothermal), stranded natural gas, and nuclear. Core products include Crusoe Cloud (IaaS for high-performance AI compute with NVIDIA/AMD GPUs) and Managed Inference (PaaS for AI model deployment). The business model focuses on an energy-first approach: sourcing low-cost power, manufacturing key components in-house, and providing scalable AI cloud services to address compute and energy constraints for AI workloads.
